# Key Notes 
----- 
**AuRoClean™** is an Acronym for "**Au**tonomous **Ro**bot for **Clean**ing", Initially made in collaboration with BARC in ROS(1) framework, with minimal Autonomous Behaviour[^1].
Current version of AuRoClean™ is developed on the **ROS2** middleware with **Nav2** and **slam_toolbox** for complete autonomous operation of the robot.
